Seraphim messenger


seraphim.online

platforms:

Corporate messenger with protection against interception of messages and leakage of confidential information. The product with an unprecedented level of security.

tools:
Jira - Figma - Miro - Zeplin - GitHub - Framer - Amplitude

First of all, I organized the correct architecture and design system of the project, which made life much easier for designers and developers. I built interaction processes so that developers enjoy, and do not swear, when new features are introduced. The designers worked, not delved into the system. Of course, I did not forget about the all-seeing management, which admires the speed of implementation of tasks.

The project runs on three platforms: iOS, Android, and Desktop. It was important to ensure a consistent design. Sometimes we should move away from the usual guidelines and implement non-standard platform solutions. But usability, as they say, requires sacrifice.

Seraphim is a non trivial messenger. It was necessary to turn on all the product charms and strain the design convolutions in order to achieve the desired results.
